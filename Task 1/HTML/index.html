So both sides like jazz and Gospel. But the other side was perhaps more RnB my elder cousins introduced me to Hiphop and Rap. Then comes the year 2000 the release of DSTV. It's only when I grew older I realised we were middle class not rich. Those days the western shows were showing rock music mostly. Enter gutair hero and I feel like I'm actually playing this music I so like. Enter influence people telling me it's just a phase not knowing how stubborn I actually am. Now I listen to heavy metal mostly. Still listen to rap, gospel, and jazz. but mostly rock and roll. So heres my playlist
